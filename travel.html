Some Places I've recently been to

Since I love traveling, I just wanted to share some places I've been to recently that were really fun.

Portland

Coolest thing: The nature and waterfalls were beautiful.

What to eat: Luc Lac, surprisingly the best pho I've ever had. Of course go for the Voodoo donuts also.

What surprised me: How walkable the city was.

Seattle

Coolest thing: I got a free t shirt for walking down all 832 steps of the space needle.

What to eat: Ludi's has the best filipino food.

What surprised me: How hilly it was, I needed scooters to get anywhere.

Vancouver

Coolest thing: Biking around Stanley Park.

What to eat: I overate poutine, I cannot have any more. The pot pie at Granville is amazing.

What surprised me: How easy it was to get everywhere on public transit (includes water taxis!)

Denver

Coolest thing: Meow Wolf

What to eat: Unfortunately I did not get to try rocky mountain oysters

What surprised me: How empty it was, there was very few people downtown.

Istanbul

Coolest thing: Seeing Asia and Europe at the same time.

What to eat: Everything was so good, but the best was a dish called the Harem Delight

What surprised me: How many cats there were walking around, and they were all clean too.

Berlin

Coolest thing: So many museums in one island.

What to eat: Currywurst, a Berlin classic.

What surprised me: How dirtier it was compared to rest of Germany.

Prague

Coolest thing: Seeing the castle and the bridge, it was the prettiest city I have ever seen.

What to eat: Rabbit in cream sauce.

What surprised me: They don't take Euros.

Munich

Coolest thing: Oktoberfest

What to eat: Go to Hofbräuhaus to have their schnitzel.

What surprised me: How clean the city was.

Washington DC

Coolest thing: The museums at the National Mall

What to eat: Half smoke hot dog at Ben's

What surprised me: How bad some neighborhoods can get so close to the capital.

Monterrey

Coolest thing: The best Mexican food I've had.

What to eat: Chicharron from Carniceria Ramos.

What surprised me: I did not expect such beautiful mountains.

Rio de Janeiro

Coolest thing: Taking a moto up the favela.

What to eat: Everything is so good, but can't miss the churrasco.

What surprised me: How close the poor and rich neighborhoods were.

São Paulo

Coolest thing: Ibirapuera Park is so nice and big and has vairous museums.

What to eat: I had some great açaí here.

What surprised me: How modern the city looked, full of skyscrapers.

Dallas

Coolest thing: Buccee's

What to eat: Barbeque brisket

What surprised me: It wasn't as cowboy as I thought it would be.

Ciudad de Mexico

Coolest thing: How busy the most populated city in the Americas is.

What to eat: Tacos al pastor

What surprised me: How close the ruins and pyramids were to the city.

Cusco

Coolest thing: Machu Picchu

What to eat: Cuy

What surprised me: How well kept cusco is, still looks like an Incan village.

Panama City

Coolest thing: Seeing the canal in action

What to eat: Codfish fritters from La Tapa del Coco

What surprised me: They use dollar bills but Panamanian coins.

New Orleans

Coolest thing: Live Jazz everywhere

What to eat: Gumbo and jambalaya

What surprised me: How much music there was.
